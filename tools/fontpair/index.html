Finding the Perfect Pairing


                
The key to successful font pairing lies in contrast. Combining a serif heading with a sans-serif body creates visual interest while maintaining readability. The heading draws attention while the body text recedes, allowing the content to shine.


                
"Typography is the craft of endowing human language with a durable visual form."

Key Principles


                
When selecting fonts, consider the following guidelines:


                
    
                  
  • Contrast: Pair fonts with different characteristics
    • 
                  
  • Hierarchy: Use size and weight to establish importance
    • 
                  
  • Readability: Body text must be easy to read at small sizes
    • 
                  
  • Personality: Fonts should match your brand's voice
    • 
                


                
Remember that less is often more. Stick to two or three fonts maximum to maintain a cohesive design.
