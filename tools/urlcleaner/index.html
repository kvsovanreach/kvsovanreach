About Tracking Parameters

Tracking parameters are added to URLs by various services to track where visitors come from:

  • utm_* - Google Analytics campaign tracking
  • fbclid - Facebook click identifier
  • gclid - Google Ads click identifier
  • ref - Referral source tracking

Privacy tip: Removing these parameters helps protect your privacy when sharing links.
