About Stopwords

Stopwords are common words that carry little meaning on their own, like "the", "is", "at", etc. Removing them is useful for:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Text analysis and mining
  • Search engine optimization
  • Reducing noise in datasets

Note: All processing happens locally. Your text is never sent to any server.
