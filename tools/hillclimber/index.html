How to Play
  1. Click on the terrain to place the ball
  2. Adjust the sliders to control the physics
  3. Guide the ball to the lowest point (global minimum)
  4. The ball must stay near the minimum when time runs out

Tip: High learning rate = faster but may overshoot. High momentum = smoother but harder to stop.
