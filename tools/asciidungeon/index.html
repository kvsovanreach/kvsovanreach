How to Play
  1. Navigate the dungeon using Arrow Keys or WASD
  2. Collect gold ($) and health potions (+)
  3. Attack enemies by moving into them
  4. Find the stairs (>) to descend deeper
  5. Survive as long as you can!

Tip: Enemies move after you do. Plan your path carefully and don't get surrounded!
