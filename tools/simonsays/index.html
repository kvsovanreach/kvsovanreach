How to Play
  • Press Start or S to begin a new game
  • Watch the color sequence, then repeat it by clicking the pads
  • Keyboard: 1=Red, 2=Blue, 3=Green, 4=Yellow
  • Normal mode: replays sequence on mistake. Strict mode: restarts the game
  • Speed increases every 5 rounds
