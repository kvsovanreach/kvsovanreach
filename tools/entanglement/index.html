How to Play
  1. Click any tile to toggle it ON/OFF
  2. Adjacent tiles (up, down, left, right) also toggle!
  3. Goal: Turn ALL tiles ON (glowing)
  4. Try to solve it in the fewest moves

Tip: Sometimes you need to "undo" progress to make real progress. Think several steps ahead!
