About GCD & LCM

GCD (Greatest Common Divisor) is the largest number that divides all given numbers evenly.

LCM (Least Common Multiple) is the smallest number that is a multiple of all given numbers.

  • For two numbers: GCD(a, b) x LCM(a, b) = a x b
  • Numbers are coprime if their GCD is 1
  • The Euclidean algorithm efficiently computes GCD using repeated division

Tip: Enter comma-separated positive integers. Works with 2 or more numbers.
